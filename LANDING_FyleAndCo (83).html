About Fyle & Co.

Delivering Depth, Discretion &
Cross-border Fluency

Fyle & Co. advises private investors, founders, and institutions on matters of capital, succession, and strategic transition. Our work is defined by restraint, precision, and a long-term perspective that places integrity before ambition.

We operate quietly, aligning with those who value thoughtful judgment over visibility, and enduring partnerships over transactions. In every engagement, our purpose is simple: to preserve and advance the strength of private enterprise with clarity and discretion.

We bring together deep knowledge, industry insight and broad experience to help clients find clarity in complex environments across diverse geographies and industry sectors.

Our Approach

How We
Work

We provide strategic and tactical guidance across the full lifecycle of a transaction — from origination through to completion — with a team that brings together buy-side and sell-side expertise across multiple geographies.

01
Buy-Side Advisory

We assist corporates, financial sponsors and their portfolio companies on a retained basis to execute acquisition-led growth strategies, as well as advice on any associated fundraising across Africa, the UAE and Europe.

02
Sell-Side Advisory

We provide strategic and tactical guidance to sell-side events. Our advisory businesses comprise joint ventures, spin-offs and divestitures across our core geographies.

03
Capital Raises

Covering debt, equity, mezzanine, strategic and trade finance structures, we assist clients in raising the right capital from the right counterparties at the right time.

04
Relationship Intelligence

Our institutional investor network spans 50+ pre-qualified investors across Africa, the UAE and Europe. We leverage deep relationships and proprietary intelligence to connect clients with aligned capital partners.
